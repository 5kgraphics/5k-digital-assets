How to Download

Step-by-step guide to accessing our digital Assets

Download Process

  1. Browse or Search for Assets: On the homepage, you can browse through various categories of digital assets or use the search bar or filter to find a specific asset or course.
  2. Select your desired Asset: Once you find an asset you're interested in, click on the more info button to open the product information page
  3. Click the Download Now Button: On the Assets Information page, you'll see a prominent "Download Now" button. Click this button to initiate the download.
  4. Complete a quick Task: To ensure the security and integrity of our digital assets, and to prevent automated downloads, we locked our digital assets which require users to perform a simple task like installing an app, taking a survey, e.t.c This Helps us maintain automated downloads by users. Please complete one task to unlock the desired digital assets from the list of tasks that appears by following the instructions.

Download Tips

  • Use a stable internet connection for large file downloads
  • Download files immediately after completing a task
  • Keep backup copies of your important downloads
  • Use WinRAR or 7-Zip to extract compressed files
  • Check the file format requirements before downloading
  • Switch off vpn when performing task to unlock this assets as using vpn will not unlock the desired asset

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Download link not working?
Try copying the link and pasting it into a new browser window. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache or try a different browser.
File won't open after download?
Make sure you have the appropriate software to open the file type. For compressed files, use extraction software like WinRAR or 7-Zip.
Download interrupted?
You can re-download your files at any time, Make sure you have a working internet connection and switch off any vpn.
